Advanced Accessibility for PDFs
When: Thursday, Oct. 25 from 1-3 p.m.

October is National Domestic Violence Awareness Month

Posted Oct. 23

It is EPA's policy to ensure that measures are implemented to address the needs of agency employees and non-agency personnel who are victims of domestic violence impacting the workplace and to take corrective action, as appropriate.

All employees are encouraged to review the agency's Domestic Violence, Sexual Assault and Stalking Policy and take the agency's the Domestic Violence elearning course on FedTalent.

If you are concerned for your well-being or that of a coworker, say something to your manager, a Local Violence Prevention Coordinator, or contact the Employee Assistance Program at 888-635-3202, online at www.espyr.com (user name: usepa), or by downloading the mobile application by visiting Google Play or the Apple Store (password: usepa).

CFC Cause of the Week: Eradicating Poverty

Posted Oct. 22

In 2016, 12.7 percent of Americans were classified as living in poverty, including 15.3 million children. In developing countries, the barometer for poverty is very low: many impoverished people live off less than $1.90 a day. Contributions, no matter the size, can go a long way in making a difference in the life of the poor. For example:

  • $5 provides 25 meals for a family of four.
  • $35 creates a new resume for a job seeker.
  • $100 buys one toilet, two hard hats, and three hammers to support housing development for families across the United States.

For information on how to donate to the CFC, such as for the cause above, visit the CFC intranet site.

FedTalent Now Available

Posted Sept. 28

FedTalent, EPA's new integrated learning management system, is now live. FedTalent will be accessible soon through One EPA Workplace under the EPA University dropdown and in the Quick Links gallery via the eLearning icon. FedTalent provides online access to mandatory, optional, and developmental training. Employees will have access to a robust catalog of online courses developed by:

  • EPA.
  • OPM.
  • SkillSoft.
  • Other online training providers.

The Department of Interior Business Center (IBC) will operate and maintain the FedTalent System. IBC is a designated Human Resources Federal Shared Service provider for EPA and other federal agency customers. If you need assistance with FedTalent, please call the IBC helpdesk at 1-888-367-1622.

Further information on FedTalent can be found on the EPA University SharePoint site.
